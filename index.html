About Us

Frig Crafters Pvt. Ltd.

Established in the year 1977, our company 'Frig Crafters Pvt. Ltd.' is a icon manufacturer and exporter, engaged in designing, fabricating and installing Display Counters, Refrigerators and Kitchen Equipments for industrial and commercial purposes. Due to our efficient inputs and result oriented output, we have managed to establish a strong marketing arm in the industry. Our experience of more than thirty-five years has provided us the necessary expertize and extensive knowledge in the field of manufacturing, distributing and selling.

  • Innovative Technology – We integrate cutting-edge technology to ensure energy efficiency, durability, and high performance.

  • Customized Solutions – Our products are tailored to meet the specific needs of clients across various industries.

  • Reliable Customer Support – We provide end-to-end assistance, from design consultation to after-sales service, ensuring complete customer satisfaction.
